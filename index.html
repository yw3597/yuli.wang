Yuli Wang

Yuli Wang is a Chinese female architectural designer based in New York, US. Graduating from the University of Notre Dame, she holds a Bachelor in Architecture degree with a minor in Philosophy. She is currently a 3rd-year M.Arch student at Columbia GSAPP and will graduate in May 2023.

Her career, since the very beginning, has been built on an international exploration and local practice. She has gained professional experiences around the world, including at Christian Kerez in Italy, PEI Architects in the United States, and Atelier FCJZ in China. She has been participated in projects of different scales and types, such as residential buildings, public spaces, bridges, civic infrastructures, governments and commercial spaces.
